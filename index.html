Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our IPTV service
What internet speed is recommended?
For a stable experience, use a reliable connection. As a guideline: 10–15 Mbps for HD and 25+ Mbps for 4K (when available). Performance depends on network, device, and configuration.
Is IBO Player activation free?
Yes. Activation and basic setup guidance are included with your subscription (MAC Address required).
How fast is activation?
Usually right after payment confirmation and receiving the required details (e.g., MAC Address).
What is included in the service?
You get access to IPTV content (live channels and VOD) plus setup guidance. Availability and channel lists may change over time and vary by region.
Do you have sports events / PPV?
Sports events and PPV may be available depending on the period and region. Message us and we will confirm for your case.
What video quality is offered (HD/4K)?
Quality depends on the channel/content and your internet speed. HD/Full HD is common; 4K may be available for some content.
What information is required for activation?
We only request the technical details needed for setup, usually a MAC Address and “Device ID”.
Do I need a VPN?
A VPN is not required in many cases. However, if your ISP blocks or throttles IPTV traffic, a VPN may improve stability. If you have issues, message us and we will advise based on your device and region.
How does payment and activation work?
After you choose a plan, contact us on WhatsApp or Messenger. We confirm the details, you pay securely via PayPal, and we activate the service once payment is confirmed and we receive the required technical info (e.g., MAC Address).
